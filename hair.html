The Cut

The style is only as good as the cut.
Our team of talented and creative hair stylists, regularly update their cutting skills so that you can be confident you’re in the best hands, no matter what kind of hair or style you have.

Our in-house trainer and visiting international specialist work with all our stylists on a regular basis, from the youngest apprentices to the most experienced stylists.



THE STYLE

No matter what basic style suits your features, personality and lifestyle, we’ll make sure that your look is on-trend and up to date.
Our international cutting and styling coach Cole Thompson of Elevation H keeps us a step ahead when it comes to emerging trends in the international fashion capitals.
Our stylists are trained to adapt these new trends to update existing styles, in line with the clients personal style preferences, age and features.
